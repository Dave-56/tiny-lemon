Fashion brands know the drill: book a model, rent a studio, shoot for a day, wait for retouching, then hope the angles and lighting match across the whole catalog. It’s expensive and slow.

There’s a simpler path. **Turn the flat-lay and mannequin shots you already have into consistent, on-model studio photos**—front, 3/4, and back angles—in minutes instead of weeks.

## Why flat-lays aren’t enough

Flat-lays are great for speed and cost. But for apparel, customers want to see how a garment looks *on* a person. Same fit, same styling, same background across your store builds trust and makes your brand look polished. Doing that with traditional shoots means high cost and long lead times.

## How it works

1. **Upload your flat-lay product images** — the same photos you might use for internal use or simple listings.
2. **Get front, 3/4, and back angles in minutes** — AI generates consistent on-model shots so every product fits the same look.
3. **Add them to your Shopify products and publish** — no separate asset pipeline; built for your store.

No photographer, no model booking, no shoot day. You get studio-quality angles that match your brand, without the usual wait or cost.
